Korea Institute for International Economic Policy (KIEP)

I am an Associate Research Fellow at the Korea Institute for International Economic Policy (KIEP).

I received my Ph.D. in economics from the University of California, Irvine.

I am an applied microeconomist with research interests spanning public and labor economics. I focus on the impacts of public assistance, social insurance policies on vulnerable populations, and labor market issues related to refugees and immigrants.
